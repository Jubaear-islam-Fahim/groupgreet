About Us

At Groupgreet, we see ourselves taking part in all our user's life milestones: from baby showers to retirement parties, we are always there with the perfect card or invitation

We also understand how important personal touch is; br>

that is why we offer our users online design tools that allow them to customize and create beautiful invitations & greeting cards.

Express, and Spread the Joy

We help you express gratitude, the appreciation, the warmth, and the love in a way that amplifies the effect manifold. Our simple to use, easy to customize digital greeting cards service allows you to design one card from the whole group. And the combined effect becomes tremendously higher.

Inclusion and Diversity

Group greetings is driven by a vision. We believe in the inclusion of all the members, groups and individuals in a collective gesture and make sure that diversity in this regard be fairly united to make everyone a part of the effort. Our Group greeting Cards are our way of working towards:

“to stand for unity in diversity”

Who Should Use GroupGreetings

A digital group greeting card is your answer to conveying your feelings without having to worry about space or logistics. People who can best use it are:

  • Remotely working teams who need to send a farewell card to a coworker
  • Corporate businesses who want to send a digital card to their employees or clients
  • Long lost friends living in various countries feel like joining forces to wish an old mate a happy birthday
  • The entire family wants to wish a member on their graduation, birthday, child bearing, or any other life event
  • Students who wish to send their teachers a note of appreciation, birthday or any other event.

Convenience in Collaboration

The foundation of GroupGreeting is the idea that this world needs appreciation. Appreciation, celebration, gratitude, and love. The world could become so much better a place if everyone just expressed their appreciation and gratitude towards their coworkers, family members, and friends. Reminding them now and then that they matter. Our mission is:

"To help sharing happiness together easier, for everyone.”

How We Work

We Save Environment! Through our paperless initiative we aim to save the environment by saving trees. We do not only save the use of paper but also advocate planting new trees. In collaboration with OneTreePlanted.org, we have so far helped plant trees in North America, Africa, Asia, and Australia. Each month, part of revenues is donated towards planting more trees. That means that for every GroupGreeting you buy, you save trees by using less paper, and you’re helping the environment, one tree at a time. Help us plant 1 million trees by 2025 by using GroupGreeting!
